如果你沒有電腦的話，請直接上一頁離開謝謝  
  
1.在此下載SoftEther VPN Client + VPN Gate Client 插件:  
<http://www.vpngate.net/cn/download.aspx>  

[![](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-l_wX1kRu_R8/U5nfhEt6PpI/AAAAAAAAGEs/Iv6WhbvaEsI/s1600/2014-06-13+01+11+48.png)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-l%5FwX1kRu%5FR8/U5nfhEt6PpI/AAAAAAAAGEs/Iv6WhbvaEsI/s1600/2014-06-13+01+11+48.png)
  
  
2.安裝他，其中注意這裡選擇第一項  

[![](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8a88blpiYAQ/U5ngzT7gFUI/AAAAAAAAGE8/Q69ERpE0UN0/s1600/2014-06-13+01+16+50.png)](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8a88blpiYAQ/U5ngzT7gFUI/AAAAAAAAGE8/Q69ERpE0UN0/s1600/2014-06-13+01+16+50.png)
  
  
3.安裝完成後，點擊這裡  

[![](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-S9Xw_MYtWL0/U5nhInBBrVI/AAAAAAAAGFE/sKQmNuxphto/s1600/2014-06-13+01+18+44.png)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-S9Xw%5FMYtWL0/U5nhInBBrVI/AAAAAAAAGFE/sKQmNuxphto/s1600/2014-06-13+01+18+44.png)

  
4.選擇VPN Server:地點Japan、速度大、Ping值小  

[![](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-AlSVQXK_IzM/U5nijYq6IDI/AAAAAAAAGFU/vr_A-FIznj4/s1600/2014-06-13+01+24+10.png)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-AlSVQXK%5FIzM/U5nijYq6IDI/AAAAAAAAGFU/vr%5FA-FIznj4/s1600/2014-06-13+01+24+10.png)

  
5.顯示這樣代表連線成功  

[![](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KCylbIdNlNU/U5nijUDRruI/AAAAAAAAGFY/B5JYApPx-Lo/s1600/2014-06-13+01+24+10+%25282%2529.png)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-KCylbIdNlNU/U5nijUDRruI/AAAAAAAAGFY/B5JYApPx-Lo/s1600/2014-06-13+01+24+10+%25282%2529.png)

  
6.打開Google Play:<https://play.google.com/store>  
如果還是在台灣版Google Play的話可以新增無痕視窗  

[![](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ivtJEfBMT6k/U5nlSLQWi8I/AAAAAAAAGFo/zlPZDuHJ8ME/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+42.png)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ivtJEfBMT6k/U5nlSLQWi8I/AAAAAAAAGFo/zlPZDuHJ8ME/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+42.png)

  
7.右上角登入，注意必須是在"欲安裝裝置"登入過的google play帳號  

[![](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-jQc1hlKBHEM/U5nlTllrpbI/AAAAAAAAGF4/4jEQXOQI_iI/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43.png)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-jQc1hlKBHEM/U5nlTllrpbI/AAAAAAAAGF4/4jEQXOQI%5FiI/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43.png)

  
8.打開想要下載的軟體，點選安裝  
可以看到原本國界問題無法安裝的遊戲已經可以安裝了  
這裡用LoveLive舉例:<https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=klb.android.lovelive>  

[![](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-FsOxvyejm4U/U5nlS9rAC8I/AAAAAAAAGFs/h7cI76RVXhs/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43+(2).png)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-FsOxvyejm4U/U5nlS9rAC8I/AAAAAAAAGFs/h7cI76RVXhs/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43+%282%29.png)

  
9.選擇欲安裝的裝置，再按右邊的安裝即可  

[![](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-EFRVVmzslt8/U5nlSEiVLfI/AAAAAAAAGFk/668sYJ1jG0s/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43+(3).png)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-EFRVVmzslt8/U5nlSEiVLfI/AAAAAAAAGFk/668sYJ1jG0s/s1600/2014-06-13+01+35+43+%283%29.png)
  
  
此後此帳號同樣的軟體要更新或重新安裝皆不用再次用VPN翻牆
