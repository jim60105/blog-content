## 學網開MC伺服計畫─Teamviewer開VPN  
最後我用了HAMACHI...
  
  
學網開伺服沒什麼困難的，這個部分是為了讓外網的也能連進來所做的。故跟伺服器同一區網(同學校)的不用這麼做。**XP小畫家難用之傷眼注意!!wwwww**

  
1.下載Teamviewer:

<http://download.teamviewer.com/download/TeamViewer%5FSetup%5Fzhtw.exe>  

  
2.安裝他(注意不要一直下一步!)

  
**注意按照圖中紅框點選，遇到問你用途的選"個人用途"**

[![](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-OMI4-zI9nA8/UVlwFRVgzlI/AAAAAAAAAe8/6xYHnKT9iJc/s320/1.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-OMI4-zI9nA8/UVlwFRVgzlI/AAAAAAAAAe8/6xYHnKT9iJc/s1600/1.JPG)

  
[![](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-mCdtMN3F4G4/UVlwFf51KkI/AAAAAAAAAfA/k1r2mIMnE-Y/s320/2.JPG)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-mCdtMN3F4G4/UVlwFf51KkI/AAAAAAAAAfA/k1r2mIMnE-Y/s1600/2.JPG)

  
[![](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZEoMw0vD1X0/UVlwFTzo4dI/AAAAAAAAAe4/3RhY-13Hptk/s320/3.JPG)](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZEoMw0vD1X0/UVlwFTzo4dI/AAAAAAAAAe4/3RhY-13Hptk/s1600/3.JPG)
  
  
3.向對方(也就是我ww)索取伺服器端ID，填入下圖上方紅框  
4.選擇VPN  
5.連線:  
  
  
[![](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-AfEiR_bUJtQ/UVlwIBvsqVI/AAAAAAAAAfY/yzTckpY8FmQ/s320/%E6%9C%AA%E5%91%BD%E5%90%8D.jpg)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-AfEiR%5FbUJtQ/UVlwIBvsqVI/AAAAAAAAAfY/yzTckpY8FmQ/s1600/%E6%9C%AA%E5%91%BD%E5%90%8D.jpg)
  
  
6.然後會出現下圖，將箭頭所指處ID填入minecraft多人伺服器IP位置  
  
  
[![](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ynEyqLy7zBY/UVlwFwd4zuI/AAAAAAAAAfI/MUBv5SKH0KY/s320/7.JPG)](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ynEyqLy7zBY/UVlwFwd4zuI/AAAAAAAAAfI/MUBv5SKH0KY/s1600/7.JPG)

  
7.完成拉!!
